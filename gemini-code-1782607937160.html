Innovation in tele-education requires meeting students where they intuitively interact. Currently, I am exploring Roblox as a foundational platform to build international virtual classrooms.

What began simply as a fun, engaging way to connect and play with the Daroach family across the globe—bridging the gap between America and India—has revealed the immense potential of interactive, shared digital environments for structural learning.

Looking ahead, my goal is to leverage these physics-based digital workspaces, and eventually accessible Virtual Reality (VR), to deliver high-quality, premium educational experiences directly to students in rural and resource-limited settings.
